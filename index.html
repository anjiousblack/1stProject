MAIN CREATIONS:

A LITTLE BIT OF HIS LIFE:

  • 1879 - Born on 14 March.
  • 1895 - Einstein obtained exceptional grades in physics and mathematics.
  • 1902 - Published his first paper, "Conclusions from the capillarity phenomena".
  • 1903 - Got married with Mileva Marić, they divorced in 1919.
  • 1905 - Einstein postulated that light itself consists of localized particles.
  • 1903 - Two papers was published in 1902–1903 (thermodynamics) attempted to interpret atomic phenomena from a statistical point of view
  • 1908 - He was recognized as a leading scientist and was appointed lecturer at the University of Bern.
  • 1919 - Got married with Elsa Löwentha , she died in 1936.
  • 1907 - Developed the theory of gravitation.
  • 1907 - Einstein proposed a model of matter where each atom in a lattice structure is an independent harmonic oscillator.
  • 1916 - Predicted gravitation waves.
  • 1917 - After applying the theory of relativity, he discovered that the general field equations predicted a universe that was dynamic, either contracting or expanding
  • 1921 - Nobel Prize in Physics.
  • 1935 - Einstein considered two particles which had interacted such that their properties were strongly correlated (quantum mechanics).
  • 1950 - Described his "unified field theory" in a Scientific American article titled "On the Generalized Theory of Gravitation".
  • 1956 - He died of rupture of an abdominal aortic aneurysm.
